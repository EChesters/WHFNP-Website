About us

WomenHack for NonProfits is a program that aims to bring women in technology together to contribute to projects for Not for Profit and Charities in the UK and around the world.

We at WHFN write free software with open source tools. We take up projects that from Registered Not for profit organization that could potentially help your organization and in turn contribute to a good cause.
