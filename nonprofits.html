Non-Profits

Can we help your non-profit?

Let us know about a project

1: Email us

Pop us an email to help@womenhackfornonprofits.com with your contact details and why you'd like to join our group.

2: Fill in your project details

We'll send you the project information for you to choose from. You fill out a Google doc forms selecting skills and the project.

3: Connect with a team

After you've chosen a project, we'll add you to all of our slack channels. Your team will let you know what to do next.
